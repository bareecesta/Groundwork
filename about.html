Our Story

We started with one goal: to empower the next generation of baristas through education, innovation, and community.

From Bean to Impact

Founded by a group of passionate coffee lovers, Groundwork began as a grassroots project aimed at bridging the gap between education and employment in the coffee industry.

Our Philosophy

We focus on sustainability, fairness, and empowerment — from sourcing to serving. Every step is a chance to make a difference, and every barista plays a key role in shaping coffee’s future.
